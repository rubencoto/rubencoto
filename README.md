### About

Hi, I'm Ruben — a software engineer who likes building products that ship and stay readable a year later. I work mostly in **C# / .NET** on the backend and **TypeScript + React** on the frontend.

Right now I'm building **[TuCitaOnline](https://github.com/rubencoto)**, a SaaS for medical practice administration — appointments, patient records, and the boring-but-critical workflows that keep clinics running.
