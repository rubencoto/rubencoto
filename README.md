I'm Ruben — a software engineer who likes shipping real products over chasing hype.
Right now I'm building **TuCitaOnline**, a SaaS handling the boring-but-critical
plumbing for medical clinics: appointments, patient records, and the workflows
that keep the front desk sane. Backend in **C# / .NET**, frontend in **React +
TypeScript**, glued together with the kind of pragmatic infra that just works.

> 💡 *Open to collaboration on side projects, especially around developer tooling,
> healthcare tech, or anything where good DX changes how a product feels.*
